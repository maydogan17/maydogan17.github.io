LoRA Mensa Food Adaptation

Short summary

Investigates adapting Stable Diffusion v1.5 to generate German university cafeteria (Mensa) food images using Low-Rank Adaptation (LoRA). Fine-tuned on just 50 authentic photos with inconsistent lighting and composition, demonstrating that LoRA can effectively learn specific visual styles from minimal data to generate coherent and stylistically accurate results.

Technologies: Python, Stable Diffusion, LoRA, ComfyUI, FD, DINOv2, dgm-eval, Computer Vision, Generative AI

Leveraging Prosody as an Informative Teaching Signal for Agent Learning: Exploratory Studies and Algorithmic Implications

Short summary

This paper shows that prosody, such as intonation in speech, can complement explicit feedback to improve reinforcement learning, while also providing empirical evidence and design guidelines for prosody-sensitive algorithms to enhance human-agent interaction. Published at ICMI '24

Technologies: Python, Machine Learning, Audio Processing, Reinforcement Learning
